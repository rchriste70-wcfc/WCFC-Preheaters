# Preheater Program — Board Executive Summary  
**Wings of Carolina Flying Club**

---

## Purpose
The Preheater Program ensures safe cold-weather aircraft operations and
protects club aircraft from premature engine wear.

---

## Key Policy
- Preheating is **required** below 40°F
- Cold starts without preheating are prohibited

---

## Program Structure
- Volunteer-led, board-governed
- Role-based for continuity
- Two club-owned preheaters

---

## Authority Model
- Preheater Program Volunteer manages daily operations
- Head of Maintenance provides technical oversight
- Policy or equipment changes require Board and President approval

---

## Risk Management
Primary risks addressed:
- Engine damage
- Fuel misuse
- Fire and CO hazards
- Equipment unavailability

Mitigated through standardized procedures, inspections, and clear authority.

---

## Succession & Continuity
- Documentation-driven
- Annual review
- Clean volunteer handoff

---

## Board Actions
- Acknowledge the charter
- Affirm the 40°F requirement
- Support ongoing maintenance funding
- Endorse the role-based volunteer model
